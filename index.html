Why Choose DMTH?

DMTH is more than a hospital — it’s a promise to serve humanity. Offering completely free treatment, free food, free parking, and no cash counters. Only pure service for every patient.

DMTH Jallan, Gujranwala

Located at Jallan, Tehsil Nowshera Virkan, District Gujranwala (near Hafizabad), providing free treatment and accommodation to needy patients.

DMTH Pipri, Bin Qasim, Karachi

Serving the people of Pipri, Bin Qasim, Malir, Karachi with free medical care, meals, and all essential facilities — no charges, just service.

DMTH Golra Mor, Islamabad

Bringing free healthcare services to the capital region at Golra Mor, Islamabad — a beacon of hope for the less privileged.

Our Free Services

24/7 Emergency Care

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our hospital, services, and donation process.

Is all treatment completely free at DMTH?

Yes, all treatments including emergency care, OPD, surgeries, medicines, and diagnostics are 100% free for all patients without any conditions.

How can I donate to DMTH?

You can donate via bank transfer to our official trust account (details available in the Donation section). We also accept local and international contributions through IBAN.

Do you offer free meals and accommodation?

Yes, all admitted patients and their attendants are provided with free healthy meals and resting accommodation without any charges.

Where are your hospitals located?

DMTH is currently being established in three locations: Jallan (Gujranwala), Pipri (Karachi), and Golra Mor (Islamabad), serving thousands of needy patients.

Can I volunteer or contribute in other ways?

Absolutely! Volunteers are welcome to support our medical camps, fundraising drives, and administrative tasks. Please contact us through the website for volunteering opportunities.

Is there any cash counter at the hospital?

No, there are no cash counters inside any DMTH hospital. We believe in pure service with dignity — no fees, no billing — just care and compassion.
